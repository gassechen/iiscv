* Dependency Infection Test
:PROPERTIES:
:ID: dependency-infection-test
:END:

We attempt to update =CALCULATE-TAX= with something that breaks the logic for its dependents (returning a string instead of a number).

** Sabotage Attempt
#+BEGIN_SRC lisp
(make-assert '(defun calculate-tax (amount)
  "SABOTAGE: Now returning garbage to break dependents."
  (declare (ignore amount))
  "SYSTEM_ERROR"))
#+END_SRC

** Forensic Impact Detected:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE

  [LOGIC-IMPACT] ( 5 pts) WARNING: Forensic Impact: 4 functions 
  (MASSIVE-EXECUTOR, DYNAMIC-EXECUTOR, PROCESS-PAYROLL, GENERATE-RECEIPT) 
  depend on 'CALCULATE-TAX' and require review.
#+END_EXAMPLE

** Correction
The function is restored with a cleaner implementation (local variable).
#+BEGIN_SRC lisp
(make-assert '(defun calculate-tax (amount)
  "Calculates tax using a defined scale factor."
  (declare (type number amount))
  (let ((vat-factor 0.21))
    (* amount vat-factor))))
#+END_SRC

*Note:* The forensic impact warning persists as the logical signature remains critical for the 4 dependents.

4. *Style:* Potential improvements are detected in the use of =APPLY= vs unnecessary list construction.
